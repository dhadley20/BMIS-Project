About Me

This is what I do.

Dagny Hadley is a student at Gonzaga University in Spokane, Washington currently pursuing a degree in business with concentrations in Finance and Marketing. Her background in living in countries such as India and Nepal from a young age give her the unique ability to adapt and interact with people from all places and cultures.

She is fueled through the never ending learning process and strives to work with companies that provide passionate work that also allows employees to elevate their skills.

Dagny's main priotity is to work with companies that she shares a similar passion with their mission. She believes that common goals and excitement for a product, mission etc. will always produce the best work.
